2021-2022 Portfolio The Epic of Lumina

Since July 2021, I have been developing a game as a solo developer. I had to learn and improve my skills in the field of computer science, game design and development, art, and writing. It's been a challenging and extensive experience, but I have learned very much and enjoyed the process.

This is a piece of concept art for one of the boss battles in my project. Marie and Agrias face their own shadows in the Reflecting Room of The Castle in the Sky.

  • The hud is typical RPG fare:
    • Health, indicated by a heart for Marie, and a leaf-shaped heart for Agrias.
    • AP, indicated by the inverted droplet on the right.
    • Buffs/Debuffs, indicated by the arrows below each character's hud icon.
    • Enemies remaining, indicated by the skulls on the far right.
    • And damage taken, displayed by the burst and number above Marie.
  • There are also some more unique things:
    • A rank for the current battle, displayed by the bolded character on the far right below the skulls.
    • The Mandala displays its charges in the middle of the hud: three fire, one ice, one lightning, and one dark.
    • The relic cut-in on the left (displaying Panic Button). This activates during combat to show the player which relics are having what effect at any given moment. In this one, Panic Button is granting an attack increase status at low health.

This is a PDF of a character reference sheet I made for Agrias, one of the major characters (and my personal favorite) of my passion project.
